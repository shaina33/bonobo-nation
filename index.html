Welcome to Bonobo Nation!

Bonobo Nation's three-fold mission is to inform, to celebrate and to engage. As humans, we inherit a double, often troubled, history. Bonobos – the “Great Ape” known as Pan Paniscus in the scientific literature – and chimps (Pan Troglodytes) are our nearest primate kin, with whom we share almost 99% of our DNA. So what? As it turns out, chimpanzee culture exhibits, inter alia, male dominance and inter-specific violence, including the “Gombe War” (1974-1978). If, however, we were to concern ourselves with a higher degree of emotional awareness or the enjoyment of more peaceful relations, then the matriarchal bonobos might have a lot to teach us. As famed primatologist Frans de Waal has discovered over more than two decades' worth of research and writing, a deeper understanding of bonobos makes it possible to see ourselves more in the round.
