Welcome to Bonobo Nation!

Bonobo Nation founder & CEO, Bill Caruso, is an environmentalist, educator & advocate with more than four decades' experience across these three domains. He currently lives in Somerville, Massachusetts & operates an independent small business offering English as a Second Language – ESL – education and consultation services. For more information, visit www.consigliereivivamente.com and/or connect on LinkedIn.

President, Treasurer & West Coast Empath-in-Chief, Abigail Caruso, resides in Berkeley, California & specializes in early childhood education and value-added day care services. Her integrated spheres of interest include comparative psychology, language development in humans & primates, veterinary & human medicine, music, theater & comedy.

Creative Director, Executive Administrator, and East Coast Coordinator for Empathic Outreach, Francesca Caruso, makes her home in Providence, Rhode Island, where she pursues numerous creative projects – including a website for her own original artwork: facartist.com.

Web Developer, Shaina Karasin, first learned about bonobos while studying cognitive science in college. She enjoys using her tech skills to promote greater knowledge of these animals! Visit her site at shainakarasin.com.
